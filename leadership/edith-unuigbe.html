Edith Unuigbe

Non-Executive Director / Advisor

Edith Unuigbe serves as a Non-Executive Director and Advisor to Vinley Global Ltd, providing independent oversight, governance counsel, and strategic guidance across the organization’s energy, infrastructure, and institutional engagements.

Edith is a senior legal and governance executive with extensive experience in corporate secretariat leadership, high-value contracting, and board-level advisory functions. She previously served as General Counsel and Company Secretary at Nigeria LNG Limited (NLNG), where she held executive responsibility for legal strategy, corporate governance, and complex commercial negotiations across large-scale energy infrastructure programs.

Throughout her tenure, she provided oversight on Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) contracting frameworks, gas supply and commercial agreements, regulatory engagement, and enterprise risk management. Her leadership supported disciplined governance structures, contractual integrity, and institutional decision-making within one of Nigeria’s most significant energy enterprises.

At Vinley Global, Edith contributes independent judgment and strategic counsel, reinforcing governance controls, risk-awareness, and structured contracting frameworks. Her advisory role strengthens the company’s institutional credibility and ensures alignment between executive execution and board-level oversight.

Edith holds a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) from Obafemi Awolowo University and a Master of Laws (LL.M) from Harvard Law School. She has also completed executive leadership programs at INSEAD, Harvard, and The Wharton School. This combination of legal scholarship and executive education informs her measured, governance-focused approach to institutional leadership.

As a Non-Executive Director and Advisor, her mandate is to uphold governance discipline, strengthen contractual and regulatory frameworks, and support sustainable, long-term institutional growth through prudent oversight and strategic clarity.
